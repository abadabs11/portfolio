The Company

Netpulse is a software company providing the #1 mobile apps in the fitness industry to hundreds of brands around the world. The platform connects the most popular wearables, tracking apps and club technology into one seamless, personalized mobile experience. From 24 Hour Fitness to your neighborhood’s mom-and-pop facility, Netpulse creates effective B2B2C solutions to make the sometimes scary world of fitness a little more approachable.

My Role

As a member of the Product Team at Netpulse I worked closely with our Head of Product and our off-shore engineering team. Because the product team averaged around 5 team members, I had the opportunity to take the lead on every aspect of multiple projects from ideation to production. These projects include both mobile and web solutions.

I also helped build out and document the product design process from incorporating consistent user testing and critique into our sprints to creating a complete product UI library and guidelines for delivery to the engineers.
